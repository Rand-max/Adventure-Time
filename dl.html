Adventure Time : Distant Lands
Adventure Time: Distant Lands is an American animated limited series based on the animated television series Adventure Time.
EP1 : BMO
In a distant part of the galaxy, BMO, a young scientist named Y5 (Glory Curda), and a protocol droid called Olive work together to save a dying space station.
EP2 : Obsidian
Marceline the Vampire Queen, with Princess Bubblegum, travels to the Glass Kingdom, which is under threat of the dragon. While currently in a romantic relationship, Marceline has to deal with her past conflicts.
EP3 : Together Again
Finn dies as an old man and arrives in the Dead Worlds, and he attempts to reach the 50th Deadworld and be reunited with Jake.
EP4 : Wizard City
Peppermint Butler starts as an inexperienced student at Wizard School to attempt to relearn magic.
