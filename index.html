What is this show about?
It's a series of stories about a young boy and his loyal dog searching for adventure, but set in the aftermath of an apocalyptic war, of which a magical world emerged from the ruins.
Backstory
The show is set in a post-apocalyptic future about a thousand years after a nuclear holocaust called the "Great Mushroom War".The show takes place after the bombs have fallen and magic has come back into the world.

Concept Art
Adventure Time is an American fantasy animated television series created by Pendleton Ward for Cartoon Network. The series draws inspiration from a variety of sources, including the fantasy role-playing game Dungeons & Dragons and video games.
